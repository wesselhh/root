Overland Journeys

The Convergence of Two Worlds

Embark on a curated, high-impact journey through the dramatic transitions of the Western Cape. This three-day itinerary functions as an elegant bridge, linking the semi-arid majesty of Route 62 and the Little Karoo with the ancient afro-montane forests, rugged coastal cliffs, and pristine lagoons of the iconic Garden Route.

This is an intentionally designed loop that captures South Africa's grandest ecological contrasts. Traveling from the vast plains of the Little Karoo down to the coast, you'll experience a classic route that balances wildlife encounters in the bush, walks through ancient indigenous forests, and time spent along pristine lagoons..

You will witness the landscape radically reinvent itself with every mountain pass crossed, offering a profound exploration of untamed nature and rich maritime heritage.

Highlights

Subterranean Sanctuary

Deep beneath the arid surface of the Klein Karoo lies the ancient limestone labyrinth of the Cango Caves. Stepping into these soaring, dripstone-adorned chambers after a morning navigating the sun-baked horizons of Route 62 offers an unforgettable study in geological contrast.

Edge of the Continent

Standing suspended above the churning foam of the Storms River Mouth, where the wild Indian Ocean thunders into a deep, primeval gorge. This is the ultimate immersion into the raw, unchecked coastal energy and protected indigenous marine wilderness of Tsitsikamma.

Wild Outeniqua Foothills

An intimate, open-vehicle game drive, tracking Africa’s iconic giants across the sweeping bushveld valleys of Klein Brak River. With the dramatic peaks of the Outeniqua Mountains framing the horizon, it is a masterclass in regional biodiversity just hours before returning to Cape Town.

Sample Itinerary
Day 1

Cape Town → Route 62 → Oudtshoorn → Knysna

We depart Cape Town early, watching the landscape transform into the dramatic sandstone formations of the interior. Tracing historic Route 62, we stop for breakfast in Barrydale before crossing the plains of the Klein Karoo. After a traditional Karoo lunch we dive deep into the ancient limestone chambers of the Cango Caves. Settle for the evening at the edge of the tranquil Knysna lagoon.

  • Morning:
    Mountain pass mountainscape transition & premium country breakfast in the Tradouw Valley.
  • Midday:
    Traditional Karoo specialty lunch & guided exploration of the Cango Caves.
  • Late Afternoon Choice:
    A slow-paced coastal stroll along Sedgefield's beaches or boutique waterfront exploration on walkable Thesen Island.
  • Evening:
    Check-in to premium lodging & leisure dining (famous Knysna oysters and fresh linefish).
Day 2 · Marine Marvels, Adrenaline & Ancient Forests

Knysna → Tsitsikamma National Park → Bloukrans → The Knysna Heads

An action-packed exploration of the Tsitsikamma wilderness. We trace deep coastal gorges, encounter colossal indigenous trees, and marvel at the raw power of the Indian Ocean meeting rock cliffs. Before heading back to our lagoon base, we stand directly atop the massive sandstone headlands that frame the ocean entry point.

  • Morning:
    Immersive breakfast trek through the award-winning Wild Oats Community Farmers Market in Sedgefield (weekday artisanal alternatives provided).
  • Midday:
    Dramatic gorge views at Bloukrans Bridge (home to the world's highest commercial bridge bungee jump) & scenic valley lunch.
  • Afternoon:
    Coastal boardwalk trekking to the Storms River Mouth Suspension Bridge, or optional eco-guided ocean kayaking into the river gorge.
  • Late Afternoon:
    Panoramic viewpoints atop the majestic eastern Knysna Heads.
Day 3 · Big Five game drive & Scenic Coastal Return

Knysna → Mossel Bay → Botlierskop → Overberg & Whales Region → Cape Town

Bid farewell to the lagoon as we chart a course west along the scenic coastal highway. We pivot into the Outeniqua foothills for an elite wildlife tracking experience before tracing the sweeping, golden canola fields and mountain ranges of the Overberg back towards the Mother City.

  • Morning:
    Three-hour 4x4 game drive at Botlierskop Private Game Reserve tracking lions, elephants, rhinos, giraffes and more, with an expert ranger.
  • Midday:
    Lodge-side lunch overlooking the reserve.
  • Afternoon:
    Scenic drive through the agricultural farmlands and historic towns of the Overberg.
  • Evening:
    Return to your Cape Town accommodation around 18:30.
